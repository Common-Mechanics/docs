Aisafety is your daily brief on what matters in AI safety. Every morning, an agentic editorial pipeline reads 114 curated RSS feeds — from Alignment Forum to MIT Technology Review — runs them through a multi-stage AI newsroom, and publishes a ranked, scored briefing by the time you have your first coffee.

## What you get every morning

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ranked lead stories">
    The top 1–3 stories that matter most today, with a full editorial briefing, sentiment rating, and P(Doom) intensity score.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ticker items">
    A curated list of secondary stories — scored and tagged so you can filter by category (Safety, Policy, Models, Industry, Security).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Spotlight">
    A notable non-text item (podcast, video, demo) from the AI safety world.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Daily podcast">
    An audio briefing with word-level transcript highlighting so you can read along or just listen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

  Subscribe via [RSS](/data/rss-feed) to get each day's briefing delivered directly to your feed reader, or use the [LLM-friendly endpoint](/data/llm-access) to pipe today's brief into your own AI workflows.
</Tip>
